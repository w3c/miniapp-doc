MiniApps WG chartering & the TAG

  • New form of install-free hybrid application
  • MAWG will enable interoperability among different MiniApp platforms and maximize the convergence of MiniApps and the Web
  • The work in the WG comes from the incubation of MiniApps CG
  • The CG requested TAG review and made changes to their proposals based on TAG's feedback
  • The MiniApps WG/CG will do substantial research and have joint discussions with related W3C groups to clarify the requirements and possible solutions for standardization. If there are overlapping work, the WG/CG will discuss with existing groups and work out a concrete plan to work with them.
